Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Pekadi is designed to keep your information on your device and out of our hands.

Last updated March 2026

Privacy at a Glance

Pekadi does not collect, store, or transmit personal data. Passwords, vault entries, and preferences stay on your device.

1. Information We Collect

Pekadi does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

All data generated or entered within the app remains entirely on your device.

  • Generated passwords
  • Saved vault entries
  • App preferences

2. Local Data Storage

All user data is stored locally on your device using Apple-provided secure storage systems.

  • Apple Keychain for saved passwords
  • Local device storage for app settings and history

We do not have access to this data.

3. No Account Required

Pekadi does not require any personal sign-up information.

  • Account creation
  • Login credentials
  • Email addresses
  • Personal identification

4. No Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transmit data to external servers.

Your data never leaves your device.

5. Internet Usage

Pekadi does not require an internet connection for core functionality.

6. Third-Party Services

Pekadi does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Third-party trackers

7. Security

We rely on Apple’s built-in platform security features to help protect your local data.

  • Secure Keychain storage
  • Device-level encryption
  • Optional Face ID or Touch ID protection

8. Children’s Privacy

Pekadi does not knowingly collect information from children. Since no data is collected, the app is suitable for general audiences.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
