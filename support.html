About Pekadi

Pekadi is a simple, secure password generator and vault designed to keep your credentials private and accessible, all stored locally on your device.

  • Generate secure passwords instantly
  • Customize your prefix
  • Store passwords in a private vault
  • Face ID or Touch ID protection

Frequently Asked Questions

Are my passwords stored online?

No. All passwords are stored locally on your device using Apple Keychain. Pekadi does not upload or store your data on any servers.

Does Pekadi require internet?

No. Pekadi works completely offline.

Do I need to create an account?

No. Pekadi does not require an account or login.

Will my data transfer to a new device?

Your data may transfer if you use Apple’s built-in iCloud Keychain and device backups. Pekadi itself does not manage cloud syncing.

How do I clear my data?

  • Clear generated history inside the app
  • Remove saved entries manually from your vault

What happens if I delete the app?

All locally stored data may be permanently deleted unless backed up through your device using iCloud or a local backup.

Troubleshooting

App not generating passwords

  • Make sure your prefix contains only letters and numbers
  • Try resetting the prefix in settings

Face ID not working

  • Ensure Face ID is enabled on your device
  • Check app permissions in iOS Settings

App feels slow or unresponsive

  • Restart the app
  • Restart your device if needed
